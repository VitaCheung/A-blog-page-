Homemade Sushi roll

5 OCT, 2022

I just made some sushi rolls few days ago. Sushi rolls can be filled with any ingredients you like that I chose crab sticks, sausages, egg and cucumber. Although it’s a bit time-consuming, you can enjoy the making process like doing craft as well as sharing with friends and family! Leave me a comment if you like it! Check out the recipe below.

Recipe

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: 1hr

Servings: 2 (rolls)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup short-grain white rice
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 4 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 sheets nori seaweed
  • 2 long crab sticks
  • 2 sausages
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 long cut pieces of cucumber
  • 1 teaspoons sugar
  • Some mayonnaise

Directions

1. Cook the rice with water using rice-cooker.

2. Stir the rice vinegar into the cooked rice and let them cool.

3. Cook the sausages in boiling water for 2 mins.

4. Cook the crab sticks in boiling water for 30 sec.

5. Beat the eggs with sugar.

6. Heat the frying pan over medium heat, add some oil, pour the egg into the pan.

7. After the bottom of the egg has set, roll the egg into a log shape from one side to the other. Take out and cut into 2 long pieces when cooled.

8. Place 1 nori seaweed on the bamboo sushi mat. Place a thin layer of rice on top.

9. Spread some mayonnaise on the rice.

10. Put 1 crab stick, 1 sausage, 1 egg and 1 cucumber on the downside over the rice. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly. Repeat to make another roll.

11. Use a wet knife to cut each roll into 8-10 pieces.
